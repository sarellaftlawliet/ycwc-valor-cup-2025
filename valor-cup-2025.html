About the Event

Valor Cup 2025 is the ultimate Mobile Legends tournament designed to showcase the best student teams in the region. This prestigious event brings together talented players to compete for glory, prizes, and the title of champion. Whether you're a seasoned pro or an emerging talent, Valor Cup is your arena to prove your worth and unleash your legends!

Games & Rules

Tournament Format

  • Single Elimination Bracket
  • Best of 3 matches
  • 5v5 Team Battles
  • Draft Pick Mode

Team Requirements

  • Maximum 7 players per team
  • 5 main players + 2 substitutes
  • Valid student ID required

Additional Rules

  • Unique & appropriate nicknames
  • Matches will not be restarted due to individual connection issues unless there's a server side problem.
  • Any form toxic chat, flaming or provocation may be disqualified
  • Fair play, cheats, or bug exploits are allowed.
